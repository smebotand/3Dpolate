WELCOME TO 3DPolate!

- Your New Favourite App for 3D Interpolation! -
Explore Spatial Correlations in Your Own Uploaded Dataset or Play and Learn with Test Data!

The fit of different interpolation methods are compared for you by Quantifying their Errors running cross-valiadtion (aka. "leave-one-out"). This simply means that we take out one point of your dataset at a time and use the rest of the data to guess its value behind the scenes. The guessed values deviation from the true value yields an error estimate for each method (RMSE). Interpolated Values are then estimated to a 3D grid covering your Area of Interest, which can be Reviewed both in 3D Plots and Tables!
