WELCOME TO 3DPolate!

- Your New Favourite App for 3D Interpolation! -
Here you can explore Spatial Correlations in either your own uploaded dataset or built in test data! The fit of different interpolation methods are compared for you by Quantifying their Errors running cross-valiadtion (aka. "leave-one-out"). This simply means that we take out one point of your dataset at a time and use the rest of the data to guess its value behind the scenes.s The guessed values deviation from the true value value gives the error estimate for each method (RMSE). Values are then interpolated to a 3D grid covering your area of interest, which can be Reviewed both in 3D Plots and Tables!
